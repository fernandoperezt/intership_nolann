9th International Symposium on Language & Knowledge Engineering

This ninth edition of the International Symposium on Language Knowledge Engineering will be held in Dublin, Ireland.

The Symposium, organized by the Department of Computing of the Technological University Dublin, Tallaght Campus, will be a forum for exchanging scientific results and experiences, as well as sharing new knowledge, and increasing the co-operation between research groups in language processing and related areas.
